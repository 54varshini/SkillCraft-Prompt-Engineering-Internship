# Task 01 – Writing Better Prompts

## Objective

The objective of this task is to understand the importance of prompt engineering by transforming vague prompts into clear, detailed, and structured prompts. By refining prompts with additional context, constraints, and formatting requirements, AI models can generate more accurate, relevant, and useful responses.

---

## Introduction

Artificial Intelligence (AI) models rely on prompts to understand what users want. The quality of the response depends heavily on how the prompt is written. A vague or incomplete prompt often leads to generic or less useful responses, whereas a well-structured prompt provides clear instructions and produces more accurate, relevant, and meaningful results.

Prompt engineering is the practice of designing effective prompts that guide AI models toward the desired output. It involves specifying the role of the AI, providing sufficient context, defining constraints, and requesting the output in a preferred format. This process improves communication with AI systems and helps users achieve better outcomes.

---

## What is Prompt Engineering?

Prompt Engineering is the process of creating clear, specific, and structured instructions for AI models to generate high-quality responses. A good prompt reduces ambiguity and helps the AI understand exactly what is expected. Effective prompt engineering is widely used in content creation, education, software development, customer support, research, and many other AI-powered applications.

---

## Characteristics of an Effective Prompt

A well-designed prompt usually includes the following components:

- **Role:** Defines the role the AI should assume (e.g., teacher, software engineer, fitness coach).
- **Task:** Clearly specifies what the AI should accomplish.
- **Context:** Provides background information to help the AI understand the situation.
- **Constraints:** Defines limitations such as word count, language level, or specific requirements.
- **Output Format:** Specifies how the response should be presented, such as bullet points, tables, or paragraphs.

---

## Task Overview

In this task, three vague prompts are rewritten into detailed and structured prompts. The responses generated from the original and improved prompts are compared to demonstrate how prompt engineering enhances the quality, clarity, and usefulness of AI-generated content.

# Prompt 1

## Original Prompt

Explain climate change.

### AI Response

Climate change refers to long-term changes in Earth's temperature and weather patterns. It is mainly caused by human activities such as burning fossil fuels, deforestation, and industrial pollution, which increase greenhouse gases in the atmosphere. These changes result in rising temperatures, melting glaciers, rising sea levels, and more frequent extreme weather events. Addressing climate change requires reducing greenhouse gas emissions, adopting renewable energy, and protecting natural ecosystems.

---

## Improved Prompt

You are a science teacher.

Explain climate change to a 12-year-old student using simple English.

Use exactly three bullet points.

Include one real-life example.

Keep the explanation under 120 words.

### AI Response

- Climate change means the Earth is getting warmer because people burn fuels like coal, oil, and gas.
- This can cause hotter summers, melting ice, stronger storms, and rising sea levels.
- **Example:** If the weather becomes too hot, farmers may struggle to grow crops, which can affect the food we eat.

---

## Analysis

The original prompt is broad and does not specify the audience, language level, or response format. As a result, the AI provides a general explanation suitable for a wide audience.

The improved prompt clearly defines:
- The AI's role (science teacher)
- The target audience (12-year-old student)
- The language level (simple English)
- The response format (three bullet points)
- The inclusion of a real-life example
- A word limit

These additional instructions help the AI generate a response that is easier to understand, more focused, and better suited to the intended audience.
# Prompt 2

## Original Prompt

Write an email.

### AI Response

Subject: Leave Request

Dear Sir/Madam,

I hope you are doing well. I would like to request leave for personal reasons. Kindly approve my leave.

Thank you.

Regards,
John

---

## Improved Prompt

You are an HR manager.

Write a professional leave request email to my manager requesting two days of leave due to a family function.

Keep it under 120 words.

### AI Response

**Subject:** Request for Two Days Leave

Dear Manager,

I hope you are doing well. I would like to request leave for two days as I need to attend an important family function. I have completed my pending tasks and will ensure a smooth handover of any ongoing work before my leave. Kindly consider my request and grant me leave.

Thank you for your understanding.

Sincerely,
[Your Name]

---

## Analysis

The original prompt provides almost no context, so the AI generates a generic email.

The improved prompt specifies:
- The role
- The purpose of the email
- The recipient
- The reason for leave
- The desired length

This results in a professional and relevant email.

# Prompt 3

## Original Prompt

Create a workout plan.

### AI Response

Workout Plan

Monday: Cardio

Tuesday: Strength Training

Wednesday: Yoga

Thursday: Cardio

Friday: Strength Training

Saturday: Stretching

Sunday: Rest

---

## Improved Prompt

You are a certified fitness coach.

Create a 4-week beginner home workout plan for fat loss.

No equipment is available.

Present the plan in a table.

### AI Response

| Week | Monday | Tuesday | Wednesday | Thursday | Friday | Saturday | Sunday |
|------|---------|----------|-----------|----------|--------|----------|--------|
| 1 | 20 min walk | Bodyweight Squats | Rest | Push-ups | Jumping Jacks | Stretching | Rest |
| 2 | 25 min walk | Lunges | Rest | Plank | High Knees | Yoga | Rest |
| 3 | 30 min walk | Squats + Push-ups | Rest | Mountain Climbers | Core Workout | Stretching | Rest |
| 4 | 35 min walk | Full Body Circuit | Rest | HIIT | Core Workout | Yoga | Rest |

---

## Analysis

The original prompt is too vague and lacks important details such as the user's goal, fitness level, available equipment, and preferred format.

The improved prompt clearly defines these requirements, allowing the AI to generate a structured, personalized, and practical workout plan.
